Welcome to the Organic Research Centre! Advancing sustainable and organic farming for a healthier planet.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead the way in research, promote organic and sustainable agriculture, and provide resources and knowledge to create a positive impact on food systems worldwide.

What We Do

  • Research and Innovation: Conducting pioneering research on organic farming methods, soil health, and biodiversity.
  • Training and Workshops: Offering training programs for farmers, students, and researchers to promote best practices in organic agriculture.
  • Policy and Advocacy: Collaborating with policymakers to encourage sustainable agriculture practices on a local and global scale.
  • Community Support: Building a network of farmers, businesses, and consumers committed to sustainable food systems.
