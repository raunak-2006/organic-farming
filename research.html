Ongoing Projects

We are involved in a variety of projects that aim to advance organic and sustainable farming practices.

  • Soil Health Initiative: Investigating ways to improve soil quality and biodiversity.
  • Crop Resilience Project: Developing organic methods to increase crop resilience to climate change.
  • Community Farming Study: Exploring the benefits of local organic farming for communities.

Research Areas

Our research focuses on key areas critical to the future of organic agriculture and sustainable farming.

Soil Health

Researching organic practices that enrich soil health and improve crop productivity naturally.

Biodiversity

Exploring methods to enhance biodiversity in farming systems and promote ecological balance.

Climate Adaptation

Developing strategies to help organic farms adapt to changing climate conditions.
